Subtlety Guide

Equipment

This part of the Guide covers everything related to your gear.

Gearing Content and System:

The main source of gear are raids. However Legion introduced additional ways to get loot one of the big sources is Mythic Plus a scaling dongeon system with reward relative to the difficulty of the dongeon and the Titanforged system that basicly enables any gear that drops to become alot more powerfull. Professions are also an additional source that helps you on lower gear lvls but are limited.

Mythic Plus chest:

If you completed a mythic plus dongeon of the lvl 10 or higher you recive a 930 itemlvl gear piece in the loot chest of your class order hall after the weely reset.

Enchants, Consumables and Gems:

Enchants, consumables and gems give your character additional smal power gains.
You can enchant your Back, Neck and both rings permanently with either stats or additional proc effects.
Consumables are most of the time one time use effects that dont sustain death with the exception of flasks.
Gems are procs from the Titanforging system or provided on legendary items in slots without main stat.

Consumables:

Food:   [Lavish Suramar Feast] or  [Feast of the Fishes]
Flask:   [Flask of the Seventh Demon]
Potion:   [Potion of Prolonged Power]
Rune:   [Defiled Augment Rune] (or the unlimited version   [Lightforged Augment Rune])
Healing:   [Aged Health Potion]

Utility:   [Avalanche Elixir] - can be used on avatar to prevend fall damage and deal some additional damage to the boss
Stealth:  [Draenic Invisibility Potion] - usualy not used as rogue becouse it shares cooldown with combat potions like  [Potion of Prolonged Power]

Enchants:

Enchants can vary dependent on your gear and the scenario.
The general aim is to get Mastery > Versatility > Critical Strike > Haste
Its recommendet to run a simulation to get the most value from Enchants.

Relics

Relics offer optimization to your artifact weapon and bring itemlvl and one(two with 7.3) additional point in one of the minor artefact traits.
The power lvl of certain traits depend on alot of things including your gear, the bossfight, the NC traits and the strategie.
The best way to evaluate those is to run a simulation.

Netherlight crucible:

Is an relic customization introduced in 7.3 that has 3 tiers. Tier one is always an flat itemlvl increse for your relics up to +15 per relic. The 2nd Tier has 1 out of 6  random light and shadow trait you need to chose between.The 3rd Tier has 3 minor traits that cant be the initial minor trait. You can only choose between 2 of them depending on what Tier 2 choice you made.
You need to have 60/63/66 in  [Concordance of the Legionfall] to unlock the Tier 2 Traits and 69/72/75 for Tier 3.

Legendarys

The strengh of an Legendary Item depends on the gear you have and is relative to the replacements available aswell as the scenario you use them in.
The assumed best legendary combination can therefore be weaker for your personal situation becouse of relics/replacements.
Simulations tables can be used to get a basic knowlage of the Legendary's strengh relative to each other.
The best way to find the Legendary combination suiting for your character is to run a simulation.
The currently strongest combination for Single target fights is currently  [Mantle of the Master Assassin] and  [The First of the Dead].

Legendary
[Denial of the Half-Giants] Strong legendary only in combination with  [Convergence of Fates]
[Mantle of the Master Assassin] Best legendary for the Spec that turns  [Vanish] into an strong dps cooldown..
[Soul of the Shadowblade] Strong choice in AoE fights. Also makes the spec play abit smoother.
[Kil'jaeden's Burning Wish] Decent trinket for mythic plus or on fights that need burst aoe.
[Shadow Satyr's Walk] Strong Single target choice that requires some practice for optimal use.
[The Dreadlord's Deceit] Is strong becouse we can line it up with our damage cooldowns.
[Cinidaria, the Symbiote] Is an underrated Legendary that is strong due to our high initial burst.
[The First of the Dead] Our strongest single target legendary.
[Insignia of Ravenholdt] Decent single target legendary that also works well with  [Gloomblade].

Gearing up and attribut distribution

With progress in your gear you will more and more aim to gather as mutch of the stronger secoundary attributes as possible while still using high itemlvl pieces due to your main attrubut.
Certain secoundarie attributes become stronger if you have low amounts of them so the best way to get information about the impact of a certain stat in your current situation is to run a simulation.



Trinkets:

The currently strongest Trinket  is  [Specter of Betrayal]. Its uses along with diffrent other Trinkets.
[Convergence of Fates] loses on value if you dont use the  [Denial of the Half-Giants] Legendary and gets usualy replaced with a stonger itemlvl Tomb of Sageras Trinket.
If we use 2 Trinkets with an on use effect the use of the first one will trigger a 10 secound cooldown for the secound one.
This means that the rotation need to be adjusted if thats the case for you.
The best way to evaluate Trinkets is to run a simulations but you can look at the graphs provided by  Ravenhod TC or  Bloodmallet to get an overview.
