Subtlety Guide

Interface

This part covers macros, weakauras and addons.

Macros:

Shadowstrike Macro:

Uses  [Backstab] or  [Shadowstrike] depending on your stance.

#showtooltip Shadowstrike
/cast [bonusbar:1] Shadowstrike;Backstab

Shadowdance Macro:

[stance:0] prevents you from accidently activating  [Shadow Dance] twice.
/cast Shadowstrike prevent you from accidently using  [Backstab] after activating  [Shadow Dance].

#showtooltipp Shadow Dance
/cast [stance:0] Shadow Dance
/cast Shadowstrike

You can add this 2 conditions if you want to,   [Specter of Betrayal] or any other on use trinket is usualy lined up with  [Shadow Dance].  [Symbols of Death] gets usualy used pre  [Shadow Dance]. This means this condition will never triger but would allow you to use  [Symbols of Death] together with  [Shadow Dance] during the animation.
its still recommendet to use 2 macros in case you dont want  [Symbols of Death] to line up or if you dont want to use  [Shadowstrike].

/cast Symbols of Death
/use Specter of Betrayal

Marked for Death / Death from Above Macro:

Uses  [Marked for Death] or  [Death from Above] depending on what Talent is used.

#showtooltip
/cast [talent:7/2] Marked for Death; [talent:7/3] Death from Above

Same but with mouseover for  [Marked for Death].

#showtooltip
/cast [@Target,talent:7/3] Death from Above; [@mouseover,exists,harm,talent:7/2][@Target,talent:7/2] Marked for Death

Vanish Macros:

My personal version of the macro, prevents you from accidently using Vanish when out of combat.

#showtooltipp Vanish
/cast [nocombat] Stealth
/cast [combat] Vanish
/cast Shadowstrike
/stopattack

Usual Vanish Macro to abuse the bug. 
Keep in mind that its required to press the key more then once for the bug.

#showtooltipp 
/cast Vanish
/cast Shadowstrike
/stopattack

The same can be achived with the abve macro but its more reliable this way.
Just spamm the macro and start using(spamming)  [Shadow Dance] when you start to fall down in den  [Death from Above] animation.

#showtooltipp 
/cast Vanish
/castsequence reset=4 Shadowstrike, Death from Above
/stopattack

Nightblade Mouseover Macro:

Use  [Nightblade] on mouseover, uses nightblade on the target if no mouseover exist. 

#showtooltip
/cast [@mouseover, exists, harm][@Target] Nightblade

remove @target if you dont want the macro to only work on mouseover.

Shadowstep Mouseover Macro:

Its often quite nice to have the option to  [Shadowstep] a target via mouseover during a fight.

#showtooltip
/cast [@mouseover, exists][@Target] Shadowstep

Archmage Vargoths - Shadowstep Macro:

First use places Archmage Vargoths image on the ground. Secound uses  [Shadowstep] on the Image.
This can be used tactical during raids or mythic plus.

#showtooltip  Archmage Vargoth's Spare Staff
/use Archmage Vargoth's Spare Staff
/cleartarget
/target Image of Archmage Vargoth
/cast Shadowstep

Itemslot Use Macro:

Uses the Itemslot, can be used for Trinkets, Rings or Engineering Enchants. 

#showtooltip 6
/use 6

Itemslots:
 1 - Head Slot 
6 - Belt Slot 
11 - Ring Slot 1 
12 - Ring Slot 2 
13 - Trinket Slot 1 
14 - Trinket Slot 2 
15 - Back Slot

Tricks of the Trade Macro:

[Tricks of the Trade] on the Main tank.

#showtooltip Tricks of the Trade
/cast [target=Tankname] Tricks of the Trade
