Subtlety Guide

Gameplay

This part of the guide covers the gameplay aspect of the the specification.
This section covers the gameplay for non  [Death from Above] Talent choices.

Non  [Death from Above] Talent combinations:

The gameplay changes abit when not playing with  [Death from Above]. 

Pre pull and Opener:

Be sure that you have  [Lavish Suramar Feast],  [Defiled Augment Rune] and  [Flask of the Seventh Demon] up when the pull timer starts.
Use  [Tricks of the Trade] on the main tank.
Use  [Potion of Prolonged Power] 1 secound pre pull. 
Use  [Shadow Blades] on pull. 

If you talent in  [Marked for Death] you have to additional use the ability on cooldown pre pull. The reason is the folowing:
Combo point start decreasing after 20 secounds in an intervall of 1 point per 10 secounds. The changes made  [Marked for Death] cooldown 40 secounds so you wil be on 4 combopoints guaranteed on pull. We start always with  [Shadowstrike] this means we gain an additional cast of  [Marked for Death] in the best case during the openeing while not losing any combo points for the opener.

Opener(without  [The First of the Dead]):


Opener(with  [The First of the Dead]):

[Nightblade] will be on 3 cp during opener when not using  [Marked for Death].


Rotation:

The main change comes from the lose of the [Death from Above] interaction that gives us more options to vary our  [Shadow Dance] and  [Vanish] usage.

We use  [Symbols of Death] on cooldown.

[Shadow Dance] is used with 4 or less combo points during  [Symbols of Death] or when  [Symbols of Death] cooldown is higher then 12 secounds (15 with T20 4p and  [Dark Shadow] talent). You will always cast  [Shadowstrike] if possible and spend your combo points with  [Eviscerate] when you are at or aboth 5.   [Shadow Dance] additionaly used when about to cap on  [Shadow Dance] charges. [Shadow Dance] is also not used during  the  [The First of the Dead] buff. Keep in mind to not cast  [Shadow Dance] if you would need to refresh  [Nightblade] during the duration.

[Vanish] is used during  [Symbols of Death] folowed my 2  [Shadowstrike] casts if possible when using the  [Vanish] bug. 
You can see the use in my  Youtube Video.

[Marked for Death] is used on cooldown but not during  [Vanish],  [Stealth], [Subterfuge](if talented) or  [Shadow Dance].  you can delay it slightly on add fights and abuse the reset on dying adds.

[Backstab] is out main combo builder that gets replaced by  [Gloomblade] if talented.

[Nightblade] is best cycled in a way where you dont need to refresh it during symbols while still playing around   [Finality] and Pandemic. 

Pandemic timers are:

  - without T19 2p 
4.8 seconds with 5 combo points
 5.4 seconds with 6 combo points (DS) 

- with T19 2p 
7.8 seconds with 5 combo points   
9 seconds with 6 combo points (DS) 

The usual rotation is: 

start -> apply   [Nightblade]-> refresh   [Nightblade] with the highest possible pandemic time -> let it run to the last tick -> start

However we can break this circle to meet the requirement to have the duration of   [Symbols of Death] covered by   [Nightblade]. We refresh  [Nightblade] with 5 combo points up to 5 secounds before   [Symbols of Death] comes up or 6 secounds with 6 combo points.  

[Eviscerate] and  [Nightblade] are used at 5 or 6 combo points all the time.

[Goremaw's Bite] is used with 2 or less combo points and 50 or less energy (90 with  [Vigor]) and not during  [Shadow Dance],  [Stealth],  [Vanish] or  [Subterfuge].

[Shuriken Toss] is only used when not you need to dump energy and have no other option.

[Shadow Blades] is used on cooldown.

Multi target:

[Shuriken Storm] replaces  [Backstab] with 2 or more targets.
[Shuriken Storm] replaces  [Shadowstrike] with 3 or more targets during  [Vanish],  [Stealth], [Subterfuge] or  [Shadow Dance].

[Nightblade] is applyed to all targets when using the T19 2p Boni. Withotu T19 2p we only apply it to the priority target. 
Only apply to additional targets if they survive for a minimum fo 12 secounds.
Keep in mind that using  [Nightblade] on multiple targets results in a priority target damage loss.

[Eviscerate] can be used on 4 combo points with 4 targets.

[The Dreadlord's Deceit] doesent change the multi taget rotation. If you use it on single target fights use   [Shuriken Storm] as a combo builder if you are on 29 or more stacks. 

Trinkets:

When using multiple Use trinkets the use of one trinket will trigger a 10 sec cooldown on the 2nd one, keep that in mind.
If you use combinations with  [Specter of Betrayal] priorize it over other trinkets and probably use the 2nd trinket on cd.

Some trinkets have special use conditions i list the popular ones:

[Specter of Betrayal] - Use when  [Shadow Dance] during  [Symbols of Death]. You can macro it to  [Shadow Dance] if you want. Just keep in mind to position propper when using the Trinket. 

[Umbral Moonglaives] - Use it when  [Shadow Dance] and  [Symbols of Death] are up. You can adjust the use in multi target situations to use it with  [Symbols of Death] and get the explosion during  [Shadow Dance](if possible).

[Vial of Ceaseless Toxins] - Use it with  [Shadow Dance] and  [Symbols of Death] up. You can adjust the use to abuse the reset in add fights.

Mythic plus: Switching trinkets in mythic plus doungeons is common practice, if you switch a trinket it will trigger a 30 secounds cooldown on the new equiped one, you can use this to prepear for specific mob groups/pulls.

Optimization:

[Shadow Techniques]  - The proc can also be used during  [Shadow Dance] when on 4 cp but i dont recommend it becouse of the tight time window of  [Shadow Dance] that can result in the loss of 1 ability cast during the buff.

Enegery Pooling:
Pooling became less important due to the increse of energy gain from  [Symbols of Death] and  [Shadow Techniques] aswell as the change to proc   [Energetic Stabbing] on  [Backstab].

Certain abilities will still benefit from Pooling.

[Vanish] you want to use it with 55+ energy.

[Shadow Dance] you want to be on a energy level that allowes you to peform 4 abilities during the buff.
 An energy lvl of 70-75 is suefficient for that. I made a calculation about it you can look into it:  Calculation. 

Utility Cooldowns: 
[Cloak of Shadows] - is mainly used to get higher uptimes during bossfights by avoiding damage from boss mechanics if possible. Certain boss fights will require you to use the ability to soak abilities. 

[Sprint]/  [Shadowstep] - is mainly used to get higher uptimes on the boss,  [Shadowstep] can also be used on bosses, adds or friendly players to avoid knockbacks. 

[Feint] - is only used if needed for a certain boss mechanic as it consumes quite alot of energy.

[Crimson Vial] - is only used if you would die else, there should be usualy no reason in a raid enviroment to use it.
