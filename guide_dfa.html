Gameplay

This part of the guide covers the gameplay aspect of the the specification.
This section will focus on the currently popular  [Death from Above] Talent combination.

Death from Above

The Dfa talent specialisation revolves around setting up the symbols of death window and align your cooldowns accordingly.
It offers you regular burst phases and strong burst damage on priority adds aswell as additional cleave damage from death from above.

Pre pull and Opener:

Be sure that you have  [Lavish Suramar Feast],  [Defiled Augment Rune] and  [Flask of the Seventh Demon] up when the pull timer starts.
Use  [Tricks of the Trade] on the main tank.
Use  [Potion of Prolonged Power] 1 secound pre pull. 
Use  [Shadow Blades] on pull.

Rotation:

The change that allowesd the spec to becom strong is that  [Shadow Dance] can be used off GCD aswell as the introduction of  [Dark Shadow].
[Death from Above] has difrent damage parts that trigger at certain points during the animation the main 2 are the initial hit and the  [Eviscerate] part.  [Death from Above] has a intressting interaction that allowes us to use off global cooldown spells beofre the  [Eviscerate] part hits and let us use  [Shadow Dance] and  [Symbols of Death] right before it triggers gaining it the benefits of both buffs without losing duratin/casts during the uptime.

Spell Queue Window:

If you have problems fitting 4 Abilities into  [Shadow Dance] after  [Death from Above] you can take a look into the Spell Queue Window option.

This behaviour can be a result of a wrong setting (usualy to low value). It should be on default (400) or at a minimum 20 above your ping.
Use  /console SpellQueueWindow X  to set it to your desired value.

The Core gameplay is centered around the  [Symbols of Death] cooldown And the alignment of  [Death from Above] with  [Shadow Dance]. The changes to [Symbols of Death] together with T20 made this spell our strongest one that we aim to use as often as possile during an encounter. That is the reason that we use it pretty mutch on coold down and align our strong damage cooldowns to it.
When using  [Symbols of Death] you have 4 Global cooldowns to prepare for  [Death from Above], this allowes us to have some room to generate combo points,pool energy, use trinkets, use the effect of  [The First of the Dead] or use cooldowns like  [Vanish].

In Detail:

You will not be on a certain amount of combo points when using  [Symbols of Death]. After using  [Symbols of Death] you need to adjust to your situation, this means you use either  [Backstab] or  [Vanish] folowed up with  [Shadowstrike] or  [Death from Above] depending on your combo point situation. You use  [Death from Above] with 5 combo points during  [Shadow Blades] and 6 combo points without.

[The First of the Dead] changes the rotation slightly. We want to use  [Backstab] during the legendary effect and prepear for that.
This means we use  [Eviscerate] on the last global cooldown before  [Symbols of Death] when we are not in a situation where we are able to use  [Symbols of Death] folowed by  [Eviscerate],  [Backstab] (with 1 or 2 cp) or  [Vanish] folowed by  [Shadowstrike].
[Death from Above] is used after builing the combo points with the legendary effect.

[Shadow Dance] is always used before the  [Eviscerate] part of   [Death from Above] hits and folowed up with as many               [Shadowstrike] cast as possible while using   [Eviscerate] if on 5 or 6 combo points.
[Shadow Dance] is used outside of the  [Death from Above] alignment when you are sure that you have 1 charge ready for the next  [Symbols of Death] use and that you dont need to refresh  [Nightblade] during  [Shadow Dance].Keep in mind to only use  [Shadow Dance] outside of symbols if  [Nightblade] lasts longer then  4 secounds.

We have the bonus to be able to play around  [Finality]for  [Eviscerate] if we have 4 or more targets (use  [Death from Above] with  [Finality] Buff up).

Outide of  [Symbols of Death] we use  [Backstab] when possible and finish with  [Nightblade] or  [Eviscerate] with 5 combo points when  [Shadow Blades] is up and 6 combo points without.  [Backstab] is also our combo builder during [Symbols of Death] when we are not in  [Vanish] or  [Shadow Dance].   [Gloomblade] replaces  [Backstab] as a combo builder when speced into it.

[Nightblade] is best cycled in a way where you dont need to refresh it during symbols while still playing around  [Finality] and Pandemic.

Pandemic timers are:
- without T19 2p
4.8 seconds with 5 combo points
5.4 seconds with 6 combo points (DS)

- with T19 2p
7.8 seconds with 5 combo points
 9 seconds with 6 combo points (DS)

The usual rotation is:
start -> apply  [Nightblade] -> refresh  [Nightblade] with the highest possible pandemic time -> let it run to the last tick -> start

However we can break this circle to meet the requirement to have the duration of  [Symbols of Death] covered by  [Nightblade].
We refresh  [Nightblade] with 5 combo points up to 5 secounds before  [Symbols of Death] comes up or 6 secounds with 6 combo points. 

Use [Goremaw's Bite] with 2 or less combo points and lower energy then 50(90 with  [Vigor]).

[Vanish] is currently buggy and allowes us to use use 1 additional ability while not breaking  [Vanish] if we use a stopattack macro. You can see the use in my  Youtube Video.
This is extremely potent with  [Mantle of the Master Assassin] as it provides an additional secound of the Legendary buff.
We also change the  [Vanish] use to use  [Vanish] ->  [Shadowstrike] ->  [Shadowstrike] during  [Symbols of Death] without  [Mantle of the Master Assassin].

[Shuriken Toss] is only used on boss fights with downtimes when you are not in a position to hit the target and about to cap energy.


Multi Target:

Multiple target change Use of certain Abilities.

We replace  [Backstab] with  [Shuriken Storm] if we have 2 or more targets as our combo builder.
We replace  [Shadowstrike] with  [Shuriken Storm] if we have 3 or more targets.

[Nightblade] is applyed to all targets if you use the Tier 19 2p bonus. If you do not use T19 2P only apply it to the main target. 
Only apply  [Nightblade] to additional targets if they survive for at minimum 12 secounds.
Keep in mind that using  [Nightblade] on multiple targets result in a primary target damage loss.

[Eviscerate] can be used  on 4 or combo points when you have 4 targets.

[Shadow Dance] can be used to buff the initial hit of [Death from Above] in situations with 4 or more targtes.
Keep in mind that this reduces your primary target damage but gives you higher overall results. Shadowdance will also rechagre faster in multi target situations this allowes us to use it more offensively.

We can play around having  [Finality] for  [Death from Above] with 4 or more targets.

[The Dreadlord's Deceit] doesent change the multi taget rotation. If you use it on single target fights use   [Shuriken Storm] as a combo builder if you are on 29 or more stacks. 

Trinkets:

When using multiple Use trinkets the use of one trinket will trigger a 10 sec cooldown on the 2nd one, keep that in mind. 
If you use combinations with  [Specter of Betrayal] priorize it over other trinkets and probably use the 2nd trinket on cd.

Some trinkets have special use conditions i list the popular ones:

[Specter of Betrayal] - Use when  [Shadow Dance] during  [Symbols of Death]. You can macro it to  [Shadow Dance] if you want. Just keep in mind to position propper when using the Trinket. 

[Umbral Moonglaives] - Use it when  [Shadow Dance] and  [Symbols of Death] are up. You can adjust the use in multi target situations to use it with  [Symbols of Death] and get the explosion during  [Shadow Dance](if possible).

[Vial of Ceaseless Toxins] - Use it with  [Shadow Dance] and  [Symbols of Death] up. You can adjust the use to abuse the reset in add fights.

Mythic plus: Switching trinkets in mythic plus doungeons is common practice, if you switch a trinket it will trigger a 30 secounds cooldown on the new equiped one, you can use this to prepear for specific mob groups/pulls.


Optimization:

[Shadow Techniques]  - You can deley your combo builder/finisher under certain conditions to use the proc efficiently. The most common use will be to wait slightly if you are on 5 combo points and dont overcap on energy waiting for the proc. The proc can also be used during  [Shadow Dance] when on 4 cp but i dont recommend it becouse of the tight time window of  [Shadow Dance] that can result in the loss of 1 ability cast during the buff.

Enegery Pooling:
Pooling became less important due to the increse of energy gain from  [Symbols of Death] and  [Shadow Techniques] aswell as the change to proc   [Energetic Stabbing] on  [Backstab].

Certain abilities will still benefit from Pooling.

[Vanish] you want to use it with 55+ energy that condition is usualy met by default when lining it up with  [Symbols of Death]

[Shadow Dance] you want to be on a energy level that allowes you to peform 4 abilities during the buff.
 An energy lvl of 70-75 is suefficient for that. I made a calculation about it you can look into it:  Calculation.

Utility Cooldowns:
[Cloak of Shadows] - is mainly used to get higher uptimes during bossfights by avoiding damage from boss mechanics if possible. Certain boss fights will require you to use the ability to soak abilities.

[Sprint]/ [Shadowstep] - is mainly used to get higher uptimes on the boss,  [Shadowstep] can also be used on bosses, adds or friendly players to avoid knockbacks.

[Feint] - is only used if needed for a certain boss mechanic as it consumes quite alot of energy.

[Crimson Vial] - is only used if you would die else, there should be usualy no reason in a raid enviroment to use it.
